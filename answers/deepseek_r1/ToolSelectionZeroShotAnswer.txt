**Analysis and Recommendations for Face-Milling Operation**

### **1. Tool Type**  
**Face Mill with Indexable Inserts**  
- Suitable for shallow depths of cut (0.5 mm/step) and efficient material removal.  
- Allows flexibility in adjusting insert geometry and material for specific requirements.  

---

### **2. Tool Material**  
**Carbide Inserts (Grade K10-K20 for General Steel/Alloys)**  
- **Coating**: TiAlN (Titanium Aluminum Nitride) for improved wear resistance in steel or cast iron.  
- **Alternative**: Uncoated carbide for non-ferrous materials (e.g., aluminum).  

---
