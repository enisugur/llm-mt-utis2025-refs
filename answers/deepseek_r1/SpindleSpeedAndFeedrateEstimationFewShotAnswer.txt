**Analysis and Recommendations:**

**Workpiece Material:** C40 Steel (AISI 1040)  
**Tool Material:** Coated Carbide  
**Tool Diameter:** 25 mm  
**Number of Flutes:** 3  
**Depth of Cut:** 0.5 mm  

### **Calculations:**
1. **Cutting Speed (Vc):**  
   - **Roughing:** 160 m/min (conservative for material removal)  
   - **Finishing:** 180 m/min (higher for better surface finish)  

   Spindle Speed (N):  
   $$
   N = \frac{V_c \times 1000}{\pi \times D}
   $$  
   - **Roughing:**  
     $$
     N_{\text{rough}} = \frac{160 \times 1000}{\pi \times 25} \approx 2037 \text{ RPM} \quad (\approx 2000 \text{ RPM practical})
     $$  
   - **Finishing:**  
     $$
     N_{\text{finish}} = \frac{180 \times 1000}{\pi \times 25} \approx 2293 \text{ RPM} \quad (\approx 2300 \text{ RPM practical})
     $$  

2. **Feed Rate (F):**  
   Feed per Tooth (\(f_z\)):  
   - **Roughing:** 0.15 mm/tooth  
   - **Finishing:** 0.08 mm/tooth  

   Feed Rate Formula:  
   $$
   F = N \times f_z \times \text{NumOfFlute}
   $$  
   - **Roughing:**  
     $$
     F_{\text{rough}} = 2000 \times 0.15 \times 3 = 900 \text{ mm/min}
     $$  
   - **Finishing:**  
     $$
     F_{\text{finish}} = 2300 \times 0.08 \times 3 = 552 \text{ mm/min} \quad (\approx 550 \text{ mm/min practical})
     $$  

---

### **Final Recommendations:**
- **Spindle Speed for Roughing:** 2000 RPM  
- **Spindle Speed for Finishing:** 2300 RPM  
- **Feedrate for Roughing:** 900 mm/min  
- **Feedrate for Finishing:** 550 mm/min  

**Note:** Adjustments may be needed based on machine rigidity, coolant use, and tool wear. Always validate with a test cut.
